Quality Work. Every Time.

Since 1979, David Hall Masonry has established long-term relationships with our customers.


Over the past 40 years we have worked on all types of masonry projects, both commercial and residential. We strive to incorporate the finest details so the outcome of your project is perfection. Our professional office staff, skilled masons and laborers are dedicated to assist in various aspects of the project from start to finish, making your project a success!


David Hall Masonry employees are knowledgeable and experienced. The estimating department will prepare accurate estimates, taking into consideration field conditions and city requirements to ensure the job is done right the first time.


We are family owned/operated and utilize the best employment, training and trade standards to guarantee the highest quality craftsmanship on every project.


David Hall Masonry has a superior reputation for exceptional performance in craftsmanship and customer service. We take pride in this reputation and maintain our standards on every project we complete. It is our tradition to continue building new client relationships and maintaining our position as the leader in customer service.


We guarantee 24 hour response on all inquiries, FREE estimates and budget pricing.
